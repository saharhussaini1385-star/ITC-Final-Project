Application Information

Who Can Apply?

Anyone aged 14 to 35 from any country. Selection is based on motivation, leadership potential, and interest in cultural exchange.

Program Duration

The program lasts 14 days (2 weeks) with a structured 3-day experience cycle in each host country.

What Will You Gain?

Leadership skills, cultural understanding, global connections, and an international certificate of completion.

Financial Support

Yes! We offer fully funded scholarships and limited partial discounts. Apply early for the best chance.
