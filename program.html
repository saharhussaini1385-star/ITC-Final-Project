📚 Program Structure

A 14-day transformative journey through culture, leadership, and global connection

Program Modules

1

Cultural Immersion

  • Local community visits
  • Traditional food experiences
  • Language & communication basics
  • Cultural customs & traditions
  • Art and music exploration
2

Leadership Workshop

  • Public speaking skills
  • Team building activities
  • Problem-solving challenges
  • Decision making under pressure
  • Conflict resolution techniques
3

Global Networking

  • Meet international participants
  • Professional networking sessions
  • Cross-cultural communication
  • Group project collaboration
  • Alumni network access
4

City Exploration

  • Guided city tours
  • Historic landmark visits
  • Local market experiences
  • Nature and adventure activities
  • Photography & journaling
5

Certification & Closing

  • Final group presentation
  • Certificate of completion
  • Personal reflection session
  • Alumni community welcome
  • Future leadership commitments

Experience Cycle

Day 1

Cultural Exchange

Immerse in local culture, traditions, food, and community engagement activities.

Day 2

Leadership & Learning

Participate in leadership workshops, team challenges, and skill-building sessions.

Day 3

City Exploration

Explore iconic landmarks, local markets, and hidden gems of each host city.
