Common Questions

Anyone aged 14 to 35 from any country can apply. Selection is based on motivation, leadership potential, and interest in cultural exchange. We welcome applicants from all backgrounds and cultures.

The program lasts 14 days (2 weeks). It follows a structured 3-day experience cycle that includes cultural exchange activities, leadership workshops, and city exploration in each host country.

You will gain valuable leadership skills, cultural understanding, and global connections. You will also receive an internationally recognized certificate, make lifelong friendships, and build a global professional network.

Yes! We offer fully funded scholarships that cover all program costs, as well as partial support with up to 50% discount. Financial support is awarded based on need and application strength.

Our host countries include Japan, Singapore, Greece, England, China, and Afghanistan. Each destination offers a unique cultural experience and learning opportunity.

Visit our Contact page and fill out the general message form. Our team will review your application and get back to you within 5-7 business days.
