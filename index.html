Welcome to GBL

Building Bridges Between Cultures

Global Bridge Leaders (GBL) is a cultural exchange program that empowers young leaders aged 14-35 from around the world. Through immersive 14-day experiences in host countries, we build leadership skills, cultural understanding, and global connections.

Join us and become part of a growing community of young changemakers who are making the world a more connected and compassionate place.
